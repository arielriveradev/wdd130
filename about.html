History

Founded in 2005, our rafting company has grown from a small local business into a trusted adventure provider with a passion for exciting river trips. Our team brings experience, safety, and fun to every expedition.

This adventure sport has been practiced since 1811, and its modern version became popular around the 1950s. The first known attempt at this extreme sport was on the Snake River without the current rafting equipment.
